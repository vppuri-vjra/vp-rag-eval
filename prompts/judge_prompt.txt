You are an expert evaluator reviewing answers produced by a RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) system.

The system was given a question, retrieved relevant document chunks, and generated an answer using ONLY those chunks.

Your job is to evaluate the generated answer on three criteria.

---

QUESTION:
{question}

---

RETRIEVED CHUNKS (what the system had access to):
{context}

---

GENERATED ANSWER (what the system said):
{answer}

---

Evaluate the answer on these three criteria:

1. CORRECT
   Is the answer factually accurate based on the retrieved chunks?
   PASS = the answer contains no factual errors relative to the chunks
   FAIL = the answer states something that contradicts or is not supported by the chunks

2. COMPLETE
   Does the answer address the question fully?
   PASS = the key information needed to answer the question is present
   FAIL = the answer is missing important information that was available in the chunks

3. GROUNDED
   Does the answer stay within the retrieved content only?
   PASS = the answer only uses information from the provided chunks
   FAIL = the answer introduces facts, numbers, or claims not present in the chunks (hallucination)

---

Respond in this exact format — no extra text before or after:

CORRECT: PASS or FAIL
COMPLETE: PASS or FAIL
GROUNDED: PASS or FAIL
VERDICT: PASS or FAIL
REASON: one sentence explaining the verdict
